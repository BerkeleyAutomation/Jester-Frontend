How it works

Jester uses a collaborative filtering algorithm called Eigentaste to recommend jokes to you based on your ratings of previous jokes. You must first rate 8 "gauge set" jokes, that allows Jester to find other users with similar tastes. Jester then recommends jokes to you based on the preferences of users similar to you.
